Important Facts About Sports Betting

The house edge in sports betting is typically 4.5%, ensuring consistent profits for bookmakers
Even skilled bettors lose money over time due to this mathematical edge
More betting increases your likelihood of overall losses due to the house advantage

The Mathematics Behind Sports Betting

House Edge

In a typical sports bet:

  • You bet $100 to win $91 (standard -110 odds)
  • True probability of winning each bet: 47.7%
  • Expected value per $100 bet: (0.477 × $91) - (0.523 × $100) = -$4.50
Long-Term Impact

Over time, this 4.5% house edge compounds:
